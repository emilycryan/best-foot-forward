Supplements are the quiet, unsexy half of recovery: the raw materials I hand my body before I ask it to rebuild a foot. This page tracks what I lean on and why.

## The one tub I'd save in a fire: collagen

Collagen is the holy grail of soft-tissue support, so it gets top billing. I take Anthony's unflavored: two scoops in water daily, thrown back like a tequila shot and always chased with vitamin C, which is a non-negotiable co-factor (no vitamin C, no collagen). I started a couple of weeks before surgery and I'm keeping it up straight through recovery, because the smart move is to stock the shelves before the rebuild.
